How to automate/simplify programming?

Home Projects Blog
Dong Chen | Galios Lab, Huawei

Writing a correct, efficient program to solve a specific problem is not an easy task. Though programming is no longer by punched taps, it remains difficult. The overwhelming details for correct and efficient code can easily drain a programmer's attention. I believe this is one of the major bottlenecks to unleashing the power of programmable machines to solve real-world problems.

I am interested in anything that could advance the task of automating/simplifying programming. I believe the key to this task is to let programs understand, write, and rewrite programs.
